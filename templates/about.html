About Resume Analyzer

The Resume Analyzer is a Flask-based web application that allows users to upload their resumes in PDF format. It extracts the text from the document, analyzes the contents, and recommends suitable job roles based on the skills, education, and experience mentioned in the resume.

The goal is to assist job seekers and HR professionals in matching resumes with relevant job profiles quickly and efficiently. With modern web UI, animations, and secure login features, the system is both user-friendly and powerful.

The analyzer uses libraries like PyMuPDF for parsing PDF content and simple logic to map keywords to job suggestions.
